Spring 2015 Recruitment Schedule

The schedule below includes the events hosted by the Interfraternity Council and member chapters for Spring 2015 recruitment. Please be sure to visit the eligibility page to make sure that you have completed all the requirements to be eligible to receive a bid on Friday. All students should bring their UK Student ID to information sessions and White Hall Nights.

Pre-Recruitment Events

January 17th

Recruitment Info Session

White Hall Classroom Building 118 at 5:30pm

January 19th

White Hall Night Part 1

White Hall Night is an opportunity to meet all 21 Interfraternity Council chapters as well as several fraternities from the National Pan-Hellenic Council and Beta Upsilon Chi-Brothers Under Christ. White Hall Classroom Building Second and Third Floors from 6:00-8:00pm

January 20th

Fraternity and Sorority Life Info Session

Learn more about fraternity and sorority life at the University of Kentucky including information about membership expectations and benefits, recruitment, and the UK Fraternity and Sorority Governing Councils. Worsham Theater at 6:00pm

White Hall Night Part 2

White Hall Night is an opportunity to meet all 21 Interfraternity Council chapters as well as several fraternities from the National Pan-Hellenic Council and Beta Upsilon Chi-Brothers Under Christ. White Hall Classroom Building Second and Third Floors from 6:00-8:00pm

Formal Recruitment: January 21st - 23rd

January 21st

Recruitment Info Session

118 White Hall Classroom Building at 6:30pm

Fraternity Open Houses

Each chapter will be hosting an open house event on campus. A full listing of the locations for each chapter will be provided at information sessions. Various Locations from 7:00-10:00pm

January 22nd

Recruitment Info Session

118 White Hall Classroom Building at 6:30pm

Fraternity Open Houses

Each chapter will be hosting an open house event on campus. A full listing of the locations for each chapter will be provided at information sessions. Various Locations from 7:00-10:00pm

January 23rd

Bid Day

Bids may be formally extended by each chapter beginning at 9:00am.
