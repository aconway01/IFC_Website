Chapter Bio:
National History:
Delta Tau Delta Fraternity was founded in 1858 at Bethany College in Virginia.

Chapter History:
Founded May 10, 1924. Delta Epsilon Chapter has the longest continuous charter of all Delta Tau Delta Chapters in the Southern Division. Delta Epsilon carries a long standing tradition with notable alumni including Governors Ernie Fletcher and Steve Beshear.
